In Persuit of Honor

By Eduardo Cárdenas at November 14, 2020

This has been a rough year for the entire world, I am thankful the only thing my family and I have faced is boredom and not a really contagious and killer virus or even worst, unemployment. In this boredom is where this website was conceived, my ability to procrastinate has been increasing with the pandemic, but I knew I had to do something with this spare time apart from school.

Having your own place on the internet feels great and somehow surreal, I think everyone should have their own website no matter the age. This first entry is going to be short and concise since I don't have much to say other than there is for sure so much to do here, and I would love for you, whoever you are, to stick around and see this “tree” grow to its fullest height.
